Worried about having too many vim mappings and overwriting one of them? Here is your solution! A visual map to your vim mappings.
 

How To Use:
Open Vim
:redir! > vim_maps.txt
:map
Press Enter Until Finished
:map!
Press Enter Until Finished
:redir END
Open vim_maps.txt
Paste in mappings to left inputbox
